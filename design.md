# Ksenia Star Tarot - Design Style Guide

## Design Philosophy

**Visual Language**: Mystical elegance meets professional trustworthiness. The design evokes ancient wisdom through modern sophistication, creating an atmosphere of spiritual sanctuary while maintaining credibility and accessibility.

**Color Palette**: 
- **Primary Dark Purple**: #2D1B45 (deep, mystical purple for backgrounds)
- **Secondary Navy Blue**: #1A2B4C (rich, trustworthy navy for accents)
- **Accent Gold**: #D4AF37 (luxurious gold for highlights and CTAs)
- **Supporting Cream**: #F7F3E9 (soft cream for text contrast)
- **Subtle Silver**: #C0C0C0 (metallic silver for secondary elements)

**Typography**:
- **Display Font**: "Cinzel" - elegant serif for headings, evokes ancient wisdom
- **Body Font**: "Inter" - clean, readable sans-serif for content
- **Accent Font**: "Playfair Display" - sophisticated serif for quotes and special text

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Background & Atmosphere
- **Primary Background**: Deep gradient from dark purple to navy blue
- **Particle System**: Subtle floating golden particles using p5.js
- **Starry Overlay**: Twinkling star field animation across sections
- **Aurora Flow**: Gentle color shifts creating mystical ambiance

### Header Effects
- **Golden Glow Animation**: Main headings pulse with ethereal golden light
- **Typewriter Reveal**: Welcome messages appear with mystical timing
- **Color Cycling**: Key phrases transition through purple → gold → navy
- **Floating Elements**: Subtle movement on decorative symbols

### Interactive Elements
- **Card Hover Magic**: Tarot cards lift with golden glow and shadow expansion
- **Button Transformations**: CTAs morph with light rays on hover
- **Ripple Effects**: Click interactions create expanding golden circles
- **Smooth Transitions**: All interactions use 300ms ease-in-out timing

### Image Treatments
- **Hero Images**: Ken Burns effect with subtle zoom and mystical overlay
- **Card Animations**: 3D flip effects with golden light reveals
- **Symbol Integration**: Ancient mystical symbols subtly integrated throughout
- **Infinite Carousels**: Continuous scrolling mystical imagery

### Scroll Motion
- **Gentle Reveals**: Content fades in with 20px upward movement
- **Parallax Layers**: Background elements move at different speeds
- **Staggered Animations**: Cards and elements appear in sequence
- **Smooth Scrolling**: Enhanced scroll behavior throughout site

### Libraries & Implementation
- **Anime.js**: Card animations, text reveals, and micro-interactions
- **p5.js**: Particle systems and starry background effects
- **Typed.js**: Typewriter effects for mystical text reveals
- **Splide**: Smooth carousels for testimonials and card displays
- **Custom CSS**: Gradient animations and hover effects

## Content Styling Guidelines

### Professional Trust Elements
- Clean, readable layouts with generous white space
- Consistent spacing using 8px grid system
- Professional photography and authentic imagery
- Clear hierarchy with proper contrast ratios (4.5:1 minimum)

### Mystical Atmosphere
- Subtle animations that don't distract from content
- Golden accents that guide the eye naturally
- Symbolic elements that enhance rather than overwhelm
- Soft lighting effects that create warmth and invitation

### Mobile-First Approach
- Responsive design ensuring mystical feel on all devices
- Touch-friendly interactive elements
- Optimized animations for mobile performance
- Consistent experience across screen sizes
