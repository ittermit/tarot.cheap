Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are your readings?

My readings are highly intuitive and based on years of experience. However, tarot should be used as a guide for self-reflection and decision-making, not as absolute prediction. The accuracy often depends on your openness to the messages received.

How long does a reading take?

Reading times vary by type: Yes/No readings take 15-30 minutes, while in-depth readings can take 45-60 minutes. I'll provide an estimated timeframe when you book your specific reading type.

Can I ask about someone else?

Yes, you can ask about others, but I focus on how their energy affects you and what you need to know for your own growth. I don't provide readings that would violate someone's privacy or free will.

What if I get a reading I don't like?

Tarot reveals what you need to know, not necessarily what you want to hear. Even challenging messages are opportunities for growth and positive change. I deliver all messages with compassion and constructive guidance.
