Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a reading?

The easiest way is through my Etsy shop at kseniastar.etsy.com. You can browse all available readings, select your preferred type, and book directly. I typically respond within 24 hours to confirm your booking.

What information do I need to provide?

For most readings, I'll need your name and specific question or area of focus. For relationship readings, I may need the other person's first name. The more specific your question, the more detailed your reading will be.

How are readings delivered?

Most readings are delivered via email with a detailed written interpretation, photos of your card spread, and any additional insights. Some readings may include video or audio components depending on the type selected.

What's your turnaround time?

Standard readings are typically delivered within 2-3 business days. Rush orders are available for an additional fee. I'll always communicate the expected delivery time when you book.

Do you offer refunds?

I stand behind the quality of my readings. If you're not satisfied with your reading, please contact me within 48 hours of delivery. I'll work with you to address any concerns or provide clarification.
